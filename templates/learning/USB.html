What is USB?

USB stands for Universal Serial Bus and is a standard that was established in the 1990's.

We now see the USB used for the standard across most medians for its reliablilty and versitility such as:

  • connecting peripherals to a computer.
  • transfering data between devices.
  • charging devices from a power source.
